Why mulching?

Mulching shreds vegetation in place, leaving a protective layer that returns nutrients to the soil and reduces erosion. It’s faster than hand‑clearing and less disruptive than dozer work.

  • Minimal ground disturbance
  • No brush piles or hauling
  • Immediate, clean results
